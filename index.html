Welcome to LiveEdu Application

Welcome to LiveEdu, the revolutionary application that redefines education management. LiveEdu is much more than just an app; it's an essential companion for schools worldwide. It provides a comprehensive solution to simplify and enhance the management of all aspects of school life, from students and teachers to administrative staff.

LiveEdu is designed to be at the forefront of technological innovation in the education sector. Our history

LiveEdu is a web application 5 years old, which has undergone many updates mainly at the request of the users of the application

2022-2023

Addition of management of absence hours and monitoring of student tuition fees. Thus each bulletin now includes the number of hours of unjustified absences and at any time, the cash register department can obtain in one click the list of insolvent students by class.

2021-2022

The addition of editing of report cards by student, in addition to editing by classroom. Generation of official exam results graphs, sequential performance graphs by classes and by students .

2020-2021

Addition of student profile image and teacher profile. Each teacher can now access the application with authorization from the administration.

2019-2020

Integration of student ranks in the report cards and creation of a version of the report card according to the language of the section (English or French speaking)

2018-2019

Adding pagination, modification, generation of minutes, and searches for evaluations according to sequences, classes, subjects.

2017-2018

Birth and implementation of LiveEdu, specialized software for the management of student grades from registration to the generation of grade reports within the Bethesda Bilingual Secondary Institute

Choose Your Plan

At LiveEdu, we believe in flexibility and adaptability to cater to the unique needs of each educational institution. That's why we offer a range of pricing plans for our application, giving our users the freedom to select the one that best suits their requirements.

Our pricing plans vary based on the number of students in your institution, ensuring fair and personalized pricing. Prices start at 170,000 FCFA per year and can go up to 370,000 FCFA per year, depending on the size of your institution and specific needs.

It's important to note that, in addition to the application subscription fees, each user will also be responsible for hosting fees on our CLOUD server, which average around 10,000 FCFA per month. These fees ensure the continuous availability of your data and the smooth operation of our application.

We understand that each institution is unique, which is why we empower you to choose the plan that best fits your needs and budget. Our team is here to guide you through the plan selection and customization process, ensuring you have the best possible experience with LiveEdu.
