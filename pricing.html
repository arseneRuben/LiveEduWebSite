Pricing

Choose Your Plan

At LiveEdu, we believe in flexibility and adaptability to cater to the unique needs of each educational institution. That's why we offer a range of pricing plans for our application, giving our users the freedom to select the one that best suits their requirements.

Our pricing plans vary based on the number of students in your institution, ensuring fair and personalized pricing. Prices start at 170,000 FCFA per year and can go up to 370,000 FCFA per year, depending on the size of your institution and specific needs.

It's important to note that, in addition to the application subscription fees, each user will also be responsible for hosting fees on our CLOUD server, which average around 10,000 FCFA per month. These fees ensure the continuous availability of your data and the smooth operation of our application.

We understand that each institution is unique, which is why we empower you to choose the plan that best fits your needs and budget. Our team is here to guide you through the plan selection and customization process, ensuring you have the best possible experience with LiveEdu.
